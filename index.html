Pycamp: Python Classes

When I was in 8th grade, I initiated PyCamp, a Python programming course designed to introduce kids to coding. My motivation stemmed from a desire to share my passion for programming with my peers, making technology more accessible and demystifying the coding process. Over several weeks, I covered essential Python concepts like variables, loops, conditionals, and functions, ensuring that even absolute beginners could follow along. The course was so impactful that my teacher joined the sessions to learn alongside the students. PyCamp was more than just a teaching experience; it was a way for me to give back to my community and inspire others to embrace the world of coding.
